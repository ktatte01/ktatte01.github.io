Kelly Tattersall

Biography

Kelly Lynn Tattersall was born on July 17th, 1998 at Danbury Hospital in Danbury, Connecticut. She has lived in the city of Danbury for her entire life with her parents and elder brother Josh. Kelly graduated from Danbury High School in 2016 as a High Honors Student, Varsity Track and Field scholar athlete, and contributing member of various student organizations at the school. Kelly opted to attend Tufts University, where she began her pursuit of an undergraduate degree in the fall of 2016. In April of 2017, she declared two majors in the school of Arts and Science in Computer Science and Cognitive Brain Science. Currently, Kelly holds a position as a software development intern at Systems Engineering located in Portland, Maine. Kelly is on track to graduate with a Bachelor's Degree in May of 2020 and looks forward to the years that follow the acquisition of this degree.
